SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS


DO'S:
1. Keep your profiles professional and up to date
2. Think before you post — everything leaves a digital footprint
3. Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
4. Engage positively and respectfully with others online
5. Use social media to network with professionals and peers


DON'TS:
1. Don't share personal information like address or phone number
2. Don't post anything you wouldn't want an employer to see
3. Don't click on suspicious links or pop-ups
4. Don't share your passwords or OTPs with anyone
5. Don't spread unverified news or misinformation
